Biography

Tashana ZM has been singing for as long as she can remember, performing at school concerts, church events, and larger stages. She began writing music at a young age and first stepped into a studio to record covers. Her early studio endeavors included a cover of Justin Bieber's 'Holy' featuring Chance the Rapper, as well as 'Bloody Samaritan' by Ayra Starr

Tashana later made her debut in the industry with her original music through an EP titled 'Rebirth'. The songs on the EP were written way back in 2018 and 2019, and it was released in 2022. Although it was an experience for her, over time her music has evolved, and she is now set to release new music very soon.
