About My Leadership

As President of the Zambia Institute of Marketing CBU Chapter, I am honored to lead a vibrant student community dedicated to advancing marketing excellence at Copperbelt University. My role encompasses strategic student leadership, professional development initiatives, and creating opportunities for members to grow into future marketing professionals.

My vision for the chapter centers on bridging the gap between academic knowledge and real-world marketing practice. I work closely with fellow students, faculty advisors, and industry professionals to organize workshops, networking events, and practical training sessions that equip our members with the skills demanded by today's dynamic marketing landscape.

Beyond organizing events, I am deeply committed to member engagement and chapter growth. Whether it's building industry partnerships, creating mentorship opportunities, or advocating for student interests, my goal is to ensure the ZIM CBU Chapter continues to be a premier platform for marketing students to learn, network, and excel.
